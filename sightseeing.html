Tung Fort

Tung Fort is a hill fort in Pune. Since Pawna was built, it is now surrounded by water on three sides. One can ferry across in a boat from Pawna dam to its base village, Tungi.

Lohagad Fort

Lohagad (iron fort) is one of the many hill forts of Maharashtra. Situated close to the hill station Lonavala and 52 km (32 mi) northwest of Pune, Lohagad rises to an elevation of 1,033 m (3,389 ft) above sea level.

Bhaja Caves

Bhaja Caves is a group of 22 rock-cut caves dating back to the 2nd century BC located in the city of Pune. The most impressive monument is the large shrine — chaityagriha — with an open, horseshoe-arched entrance.
